About This App

This COVID-19 Tracker provides real-time global and country-level statistics on the pandemic, including total cases, deaths, recoveries, and daily updates. Data is sourced from the free, reliable disease.sh API.

Our goal is to offer an accessible, user-friendly tool to keep you informed. The app features interactive charts, a responsive design for all devices, and a clean, modern look.
